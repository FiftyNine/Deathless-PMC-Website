Deathless Conflict Solutions

is a Private Security Group operating off planet Koschei.

Deathless offer a wide variety of services ranging from installation of anti-intruder devices in homes and offices to system wide military conflicts. Plainclothes bodyguard services, rescue and retrieval, trade routes protection, riot control, law enforcement, city scale peacekeeping operations, security escort on hazardous expeditions into the fringes of the galaxy - there is no task Deathless can't take1.

Deathless is number one security provider in Acheron Rho. We are unrivaled in both contract volume and success rate. We take every contract seriously and apply every effort in order to fulfill our part. If Deathless can't do it, no one can.

Koschei The Deathless (Кощей Бессмертный)

Deathless take their name after the founder and the first president of the group, who in turn got his call sign from Koschei The Deathless - a figure in Slavic folklore, an immortal wizard king who has separated his death from his body and thus cannot be killed by any conventional means. Myths hold it that he who finds Koschei’s death shall have the power to command the demon king and bend Koschei to his will.

It’s been lost to centuries how Deathless’ founder got this nickname. Some say it was their ability to get through the most fierce firefights without as much as a scratch, others ascribe it to supernatural powers, what we now know as MES, some even claim that Koschei indeed cheated Death and still presides over The Deathless.

Little is known or remembered about this person - their name, face or history. Indeed, even their gender is called into question any time the old argument starts anew. One thing is clear, among Deathless Koschei the founder has now become a character more mythical and enigmatic than even their namesake.
