Deathless Conflict Solutions

is a Private Security Group operating off planet Koschei. Organization is led by two main councils. First one is the board of VPs who handle administrative, financial and legal aspects of running a company. The other one is so called "War Room" - an assembly of offices and unit commanders. War Room handles the other side of Deathless business.

Deathless offer a wide variety of services ranging from installation of anti-intruder devices in homes and offices to system wide military conflicts. Plainclothes bodyguard services, rescue and retrieval, trade routes protection, riot control, law enforcement, city scale peacekeeping operations, security escort on hazardous expeditions into the fringes of the galaxy - there is no task Deathless can't take1.

Deathless is number one security provider in Acheron Rho. We are unrivaled in both contract volume and success rate. We take every contract seriously and apply every effort in order to fulfill our part. If Deathless can't do it, no one can.

Koschei The Deathless (Кощей Бессмертный)

One of the theories hypothesizes Deathless take their name after the first commander of the group, who in turn got his call sign from Koschei The Deathless - a figure in Slavic folklore, an immortal wizard king who has separated his death from his body and thus cannot be killed by any conventional means. Myths hold it that he who finds Koschei’s death shall have the power to command the demon king and bend Koschei to his will.

It’s been lost to centuries how Deathless’ founder got this nickname. Some say it was their ability to get through the most fierce firefights without as much as a scratch, others ascribe it to supernatural powers or MES, some even claim that Koschei indeed cheated Death and still presides over The Deathless.

Little is known or remembered about this person - their name, face or history. Indeed, even their gender is called into question any time the old argument starts anew. One thing is clear, among Deathless Koschei the founder has now become a character more mythical and enigmatic than even their namesake.

History

The Deathless PMC was formed in 2932, during the Second Imperial Civil War and directly after The Burning Of Thorg, although origins of the company can be traced back to Aquilian legions and even further all the way to the colonization of the sector. While much of such remains unconfirmed by the official historical records, some evidence points to Deathless being formed from the remains of Scorpio Legion of House Aquila. The Legion itself had been around since the forming of the Houses and was founded around a military unit instrumental in conquering the Ragnhil system during The War For Human Prosperity. Of course all these connections are shaky at best since officially Scorpio Legion was destroyed providing cover for the retreating Imperial forces during The Battle of Isadva.

In the wake of the civil war The Deathless were reformed as Deathless Conflict Solutions and started providing their services to Houses and Corporations alike. Security was in short supply in the sector razed by the bloody conflict with weakened Houses and Imperial forces struggling to ensure safety of their members and citizens. Countless deserters turned pirates, small-time warlords filling the vacuum of power as well as some nobles trying to use the chaotic state of affairs in the Empire to farther their own not so noble goals. They all became targets of contracts for Deathless Conflict Solutions.

As Deathless Conflict Solutions reputation and renown grew, we decided to expand into civilian sector and started to offer personal and business security to private clients. Since then DCS became the biggest private security provider in the entire sector. Still, as Deathless ranks and list of contracts grow so do their history of reliability and tradition of excellence.
