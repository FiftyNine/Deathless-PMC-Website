A soldier is a soldier

Our motto embodies philosophy and culture of the organization: Deathless do not care if a soldier is a noble or a commoner, whether they're from Imperial Prime or outskirts of the sector, who they were before joining. The only thing that matters is that they are a soldier.

When you join Deathless you may choose to abandon your past and your name, to rescind any obligations and responsibilities you had in your previous life. From this moment on and until retirement your only duty will be to your comrades and to our customers.

In everyday life like in battle each of us is ready to help another and cover them. Shoulder to shoulder, shield to shield we form an impenetrable wall of resolve and unity, a force to be reckoned with, a battle-forged brotherhood of fire and steel.

Together we take contracts and we fight to fulfill them. Together we stare the Grim Reaper in the eyes. And if one of us falls, another steps in to take their place.

In that we are Deathless.

Competitive Salary

Services of Deathless do not come cheap because we don't skimp on salary for our personnel. Between regular wage and contract bonuses our payouts are among the highest in the field.

Benefits & Healthcare

We employ the most talented healthcare specialists and our insurance fully covers any injuries, physical or mental, sustained in the line of duty. Dental included.

Vacations & Retirement

Every employee gets paid vacations at the best resorts of the sector. Additionally, we offer pensions and retirement plans for long-term employees.
