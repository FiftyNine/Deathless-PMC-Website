Our Operative Divisions

We have the right tool for the job

Sentinel Division

Inconspicuous and efficient, Sentinel division agents will guard your person, site of business and residence around the clock, providing impenetrable defense against all dangers of the outside world.

Aegis Division

Aegis division consists of hundreds of units specialising in law enforcement, riot control and crime fighting operations. Flexible structure of Aegis ensures fast deployment times and scalability of operations.

Conflict Solutions Division

The main force of The Deathless offers our corporate clients a military, capable of operations on the scale of entire systems. Space combat, orbital suppression, air, sea and ground combat. Deathless always prevail.
